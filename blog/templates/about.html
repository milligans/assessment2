About me...

My name is Suzanne Milligan.


I am currently studying for a Master's degree in computing at Cardiff University. Before I was a student at Cardiff I worked in various IT support roles for more than 20 years. My last role was as a Schools ICT Engineer, which involved travelling to different primary schools in Bridgend County to offer ICT support.


I have been interested in computers and computing for a long time - since the days of the Commodore-64 in fact! I studied for my Undergraduate degree in Materials Science and Engineering at Imperial College in London. After my degree I worked in various laboratories for around 5 years. In 1999 I moved to Scotland and changed my career by studying for my MCSE (in NT4!). My first job in IT was at St Margaret University in Edinburgh, as a Helpdesk Officer. From there I progressed to IT Officer to Senior IT Officer. I moved to Wales in 2006 and worked at Pencoed Comprehensive as an ICT Technician for 10 years before changing to primary school support.
